Buy a BTC Ape Punk!

Buy with Bitcoin

Get your freshly inscribed punk ordinal here with Bitcoin or Ethereum Classic!
Punks are inscribed and sent manually to your wallet at X UTC everyday, so be patient once sent!


To buy a BTC Ape Punk with Bitcoin, send BTC from an Ordinals wallet, or send us a message on discord with your sending wallet
and your ordinals wallet BEFORE sending the BTC payment.

Current BTC mint price: 0.01 BTC

To get an inscription, send the BTC price times the amount of inscriptions you would like to purchase to this address:

BTC: 3M3xyMAiz7GWAUYPSx8KFoj96aEgCVqMgE



If less than the mint price is received, funds will be returned to the sending address minus the gas fee.
If more than the price is sent but isnt enough for 2, the extra funds will be kept as a donation to the team unless you request it back on discord.
